White Space and Clean Design

Efashion Paris

https://www.efashion-paris.com/

The Efashion page is very readable, makes good use of white space, and has adequate padding between content and borders. The information is clear to the user and there is not a lot of confusing information.

Visual Hierarchy

Dior

https://www.dior.com/

The dior site is an example of visual hierarchy, as the proportion of the images is striking and provides a focal point to indicate to the user which parts of the site to pay more attention to. The dior site makes good use of color, contrast and texture to draw the user's attention to the points the company wants to emphasize.

Contrast

Netflix

https://www.netflix.com/

The Netflix page is an example of contrast because the letters of the logo are red and the background is black and this makes the logo more important as well as the important buttons on the page that have white letters on a red background.
